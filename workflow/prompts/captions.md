# RML — Caption Formulas & Templates

*Use these as starting points when writing new captions.*

---

## The Core Formula

```
HOOK (1 line — stops the scroll)
  ↓
PROBLEM or INSIGHT (2-3 lines)
  ↓
THE SHIFT (what changes with RML)
  ↓
CTA (1 line)
  ↓
LINK + HASHTAGS
```

---

## Hook Formulas (steal these)

- "Most [bettors/operators] don't lose because X. They lose because Y."
- "If you don't know your [unit size / P&L / win rate], you're [guessing / not operating]."
- "X signs you're [betting emotionally / not running a system]:"
- "[Number] things operators track that most bettors ignore:"
- "The [PHLT Ladder / RR Engine / Bankroll Simulator] isn't what you think."
- "Discipline isn't willpower. It's structure."
- "One bad night doesn't end operators. It ends undisciplined bettors."

## Platform-Specific Rules

### TikTok / Instagram (image posts)
- Keep caption under 150 words
- Hook must hit in line 1 — no setup
- End with link + 4-5 hashtags
- Use line breaks aggressively — no walls of text

### X / Twitter
- Under 280 chars for native posts
- Threads: hook tweet → 3-5 thread tweets → CTA tweet
- No hashtag spam — 1-2 max
- Engagement > reach on X

### All Platforms
- CTA is always riskmatrixlabs.com or app.riskmatrixlabs.com
- Capper posts → risk-matrix-labs.getrewardful.com/signup
- Never mention gambling, picks, luck, or easy money
- Users are "operators" not "bettors" or "gamblers"
