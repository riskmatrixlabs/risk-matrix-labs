## STARTING HYPOTHESES (Pre-Data Predictions)

Based on the 10 seed posts in batch-01 and the RML brand voice (direct, Hormozi-influenced, systems-focused), here are the predictions going into week 1:

**Pillar prediction — Education will lead.**
The RML audience (serious traders, aspiring professionals) are information-hungry and skeptical of hype. Education posts that show *how* the risk matrix system works — with specific numbers, rules, and frameworks — will outperform vague motivational content. The brand's strongest differentiator is the system itself, so posts that demonstrate it clearly will build the most trust and shareability.

**Hook format prediction — Stats and contrast will convert.**
Hooks like "Most traders lose because X. Here's what works instead." or "The number that changed how I size every position" play directly into the trader psychology of wanting an edge. Contrast hooks (wrong way vs. right way) will generate the most saves and shares because they're inherently educational and shareable.

**Platform prediction — X will lead early, Instagram will lead on trial starts.**
X has higher density of active traders and finance accounts. Early traction will come from X. However, Instagram's visual format is better suited for the branded graphics and app screenshots that drive "I want that tool" intent — so trial starts will skew Instagram once volume increases.

**Image type prediction — App screenshots will outperform branded graphics.**
Showing the actual RML dashboard/interface makes the product tangible. Abstract branded graphics look polished but don't create the "I need that" moment that a clean app screenshot does.
