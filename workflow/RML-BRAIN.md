## KEY FEATURES (for content)

1. **Bankroll Command Center** — master bankroll tracker, unit sizing engine (2% default), risk limits
2. **PHLT™ Ladder** — 6-rung session ladder, auto-calculates stake per rung, session keys so you never lose history
3. **Bet Log** — log every bet with sport, book, odds, units, result, notes
4. **Behavioral Analytics** — P&L curve, ROI, win rate, Kelly criterion, discipline score
5. **RR Engine** — reverse rug calculator, implied probability, find the edge before you bet
6. **Session Reports** — grade your session, share your stats card
7. **Share Cards** — Pikkit-style stat cards, privacy toggle (show $ or units), share to social
