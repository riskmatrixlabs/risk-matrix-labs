How to Read Insights

Every number on the Insights tab answers one question: was this a smart bet? — separate from whether it won.

The 1 rule Green = good price. Red = bad price. That's the floor.
Who's actually better?
Win Probability · true chance

Each side's real chance to win — the book's odds with their built‑in profit margin stripped out. The honest read on who's favored.

What's the honest price?
Fair Value · no‑vig

What the odds would be if the book took no cut. Compare it to what you're being asked to pay. HOLD is the book's margin on that market.

Which way is money flowing?
Line Movement

How the price has moved since it opened. A line drifting steadily one way means money is piling in on that side — the market is getting more confident.

Did I get a good price?
+EV & CLV

+EV = is the price worth it (your price vs fair). CLV = did you beat the closing line. Beating the close is the #1 sign of a sharp bettor.

Pros track CLV above their own win/loss record.
